Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about custom manufacturing, minimum orders, and lead times.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for custom products?
We are a boutique manufacturer and welcome small-batch production. MOQ typically starts at 50–100 units for custom products, but we also accept prototype orders of 1–10 pieces for design validation and sampling. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
Can you help with product design and material selection?
Yes. Our engineering team provides Design for Manufacturability (DFM) feedback and can recommend materials based on your application — from aluminum and titanium to G10 and POM. We also assist with surface finishes (anodizing, passivation, DLC coating) and branding (laser engraving, screen printing).
What is the typical lead time for a custom order?
Lead times depend on the product complexity, material, and quantity. A typical custom tool (e.g., a tamper) takes 2–3 weeks for prototype and 4–6 weeks for production runs. We can provide a firm timeline once we review your design files.
Do you offer branding options like laser engraving or custom packaging?
Absolutely. We offer laser engraving (logos, text, serial numbers), screen printing, and custom packaging solutions. We can also work with your packaging design to create a complete unboxing experience.
What industries do you typically serve?
We serve a diverse range of clients — from specialty coffee brands and EDC gear startups to automotive aftermarket companies and outdoor equipment manufacturers. If it can be machined, we can probably make it.
